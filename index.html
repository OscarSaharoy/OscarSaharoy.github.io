What We Offer

Logo Design & Branding

We have designed logos for a number of established and emerging companies, and we have the experience to deliver you amazing results while ensuring that you are kept in the loop throughout the whole process. Whether you already have concepts in mind for your logo or not, we'll work with you to create a brand that represents your company in the best light.

Working with you, not against you.

Clear Communication

Our main focus is your opinion for the design, and we will work tirelessly to ensure the design we produce lives up to your expectations. We will offer you design choices at every stage of the process and will ensure we get back to your enquiries quickly. Even after the design process is complete, you can get in contact with us for alterations or variants on the materials we have designed so you can get the most out of our work.

Creativity whenever and however you need it.

The Complete Package

When we design for you we can produce whatever your business needs: from shop signs to leaflets, business cards to email signatures. We will ensure the package we send you includes files in every format you could need, and variants fit for any use. We will set out a clear price at the start of the design process so you know exactly the value that we can deliver to you.

About Us

We are a design agency based in London dedicated to making brands shine. We are passionate about things looking and functioning beautifully and won't stop until the result is perfect for our client. We have expertise across a wide range of visual arts, and we even have a clothing range - see our instagram @heroncreativeltd. We are not the biggest design studio ever, but this means the whole team works together on each project so our entire effort and focus is dedicated to your brand. We're looking forward to working with you!
