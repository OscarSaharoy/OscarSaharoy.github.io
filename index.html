What we Offer

Logo Design & Branding

We offer a top-range logo designing service. We can transform your ideas into reality with an all-inclusive service which allows you to come to us with a basic outline of what you want: this could be just a name for your company/project and you could let us present you with options or you could ask us to produce something you have already imagined (even then we would provide options on colour scheme, fonts and even improved design ideas). Our Logos are incredibly high quality and work well with something as small as a business card or as big as a banner!
We design each logo so that it is personalised to you, and we do our research before producing you with a finished product to ensure that you aren’t purchasing something which is already on the market. We also have pre-designed logos so that you can get an idea of our work or if you wish to get something out on the market quickly and don’t have the time or funding to produce a completely customised product.

Working with you, not against you.

Advertising Materials

On top of our logo services we also offer an advertising materials service. This package includes business card design, leaflets, banners, marketing merchandise design, letter headers and so much more! We can help you put your new logo and branding absolutely everywhere, so that you’re hard to miss and even harder to say no to!
You can let us do the designing and forward the finished product onto you so that you can take the rest of the product completion into your own hands or you can hand over the entire task to us! We work with trusted partners which will ensure that we can produce a perfectly compatible product: just let us know what you want and we will work closely with our partners to produce the pieces you would like; whatever that may be.

Creativity whenever and however you need it.

Social Media

Not sure what to do with you brand new logo and design package? Let us do all the work! You can feel free to hand over the reigns and focus on the running of your company. Our out of house marketing service will take 100% of the marketing off of your hands. We can create a social media presence entirely from scratch or merely optimise the way that your accounts are currently run.
There isn’t a media platform that we don’t work on! Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn... we even do website management and content creation. Just let us know what your vision is for your social media and what your desired demographic is and let us do the rest! We can guarantee as much activity as you would like on the pages: that could be up to one post an hour! We can offer you visitation optimisation packages which will increase the amount of visitors or merely make sure that your feed looks presentable to any potential customers who stumble across your page. Whatever your needs, communicate them to us and we can make sure that they succeed.

About Us

We are two students from The University of Cambridge and Kings College London with a vision. We are both very passionate about company presentation and have come together to create Heron Creative so that we can help others bring their dreams to life. One of us is an engineer with a passion for web design, logo creation and photo editing and the other is a philosopher with synesthesia which allows her to quite literally feel the work she is creating. We are passionate about things looking and functioning beautifully, hence why we have this whole company creation thing down to clockwork.
We both have extensive experience in our fields and decided to branch out on our own when we realised that between us we had the power to create and run a company from scratch, and successfully too!
Often the hardest part of creating a company is creating a brand which you can be proud of! This is something that we encountered when bringing our own vision to fruition, the process is long, expensive and incredibly complicated. This is why we have teamed up to bring you the fastest, simplest and most cost effective branding and advertising solutions currently on the market.
Please feel free to take inspiration from our own company presence, our social media pages, logo and website were all exclusively created and maintained by ourselves.
